OUR OFFERING

Coffee Coder Capital offers a streamlined experience for our valued clients with two primary types of investment accounts: traditional and digital.

Traditional Investment Account: With a traditional investment account, you can access local and regional markets, unlocking opportunities in a variety of financial instruments. This account type connects you with a dedicated advisor who not only keeps you informed about your areas of interest but also manages investments on your behalf.

Our digital investment account opens the door to global markets, covering the US, EU, AP, and more. You can invest in a diverse range of international securities through our user-friendly online platform. Gain access to over 35,000 assets, including stocks, ETFs, bonds, CFDs, futures, options, and forex. Our online trading platform is easily accessible from your web browser on any device. For a more professional experience, you can install our G&T Investor Pro application on your PC or even trade on the go via our IOS/Android App.

As an added convenience, we've developed the Coffee Coder Capital Client Portal, offering a consolidated view of your portfolio for both traditional and digital investment accounts, all in one place.
